<br>
>Tonight there is a stir among the palm leaves, a swell in the sea. Full Moon, like the heart-throb of the world. From what unknown sky hast thou carried in thy silence the aching secret of love?

<br>
椰影婆娑夜，<br>
潮声轻叩月。<br>
婵娟有意，<br>
言世间欲语还休。<br>
<br>
天涯何处觅，<br>
星汉几时缘。<br>
相思一缕，<br>
落人间随风几处。<br>

<br>
<br>
这夜，<br>
婆娑的是椰影，<br>
微涌的是海潮，<br>
还有皓月当空。<br>
像我的心。<br>
<br>
寂静中，<br>
从天涯哪里，<br>
带来的痛，<br>
是我的爱。<br>
    
<br>
