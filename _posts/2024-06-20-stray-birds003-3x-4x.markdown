### 30 等待
<img src="/assets/moon-30.jpg"/>
<br>
>"Moon, for what do you wait?"<br>
>"To salute the sun for whom I must make way."

<br>“月亮，你在等待什么？” 
<br>“等着向太阳致意，然后让路给它。”

----

### 31 窗前的树
<img src="/assets/tree-31.jpg"/>
<br>
>The trees come up to my window like the yearning voice of the dumb earth.

窗前的树朝我探来，像沉默大地渴望的呼唤。

----

### 32 惊喜
<img src="/assets/morning-32.jpg"/>
<br>
His own mornings are new surprises to God.

<br>上帝的每一个清晨，
<br>都是新的惊喜。


----

### 33 生命
<img src="/assets/life-33.jpg"/>
<br>
>Life finds its wealth by the claims of the world, and its worth by the claims of love.

<br>生命
<br>因有求而富足，
<br>因有爱而值得。

----


### 34 干涸的河床
<img src="/assets/dry-river-34.jpg"/>
<br>
>The dry river-bed finds no thanks for its past.

<br>干涸的河床，
<br>发现没人因为它曾经的丰沛而感激它。

----

### 35 围城
<img src="/assets/bird-cloud-35.jpg"/>
<br>
>The bird wishes it were a cloud.  The cloud wishes it were a bird.

<br>鸟儿渴望变成云儿，
<br>云儿渴望变成鸟儿。

----

### 36 瀑布
<img src="/assets/waterfall-36.jpg"/>
<br>
>The waterfall sings, "I find my song, when I find my freedom."

<br>瀑布唱道：
<br>“当我找到自由，便找到了歌声。”

----

### 37 纤求细愿
<img src="/assets/needs-37.jpg"/>
<br>
>I cannot tell why this heart languishes in silence.<br>
>It is for small needs it never asks, or knows or remembers.

<br>我不知为何，这颗心在沉默中黯然神伤，
<br>也许是为了那些纤求细愿,
<br>虽然它
<br>从未请求，
<br>从未知晓，
<br>从未记得。

---

### 38 忙碌
<img src="/assets/household-38.jpg"/>
<br>
>Woman, when you move about in your household service your limbs sing like a hill stream among its pebbles.

女人啊，你忙碌家务时，身影轻快，宛如山泉在卵石间潺潺低唱。

----


### 39 太阳
<img src="/assets/sun-39.jpg"/>
<br>
>The sun goes to cross the Western sea, leaving its last salutation to the East.

<br>太阳西行跨越大海，
<br>把最后的问候留给东方。

----

### 40 胃口
<img src="/assets/food-40.jpg"/>
<br>
>Do not blame your food because you have no appetite.

不要因为没有胃口而责怪食物，  

----

### 41 树丛
<img src="/assets/tree-41.jpg"/>
<br>
>The trees, like the longings of the earth, stand a-tiptoe to peep at the heaven.

<br>树丛
<br>踮起脚尖，窥视天堂
<br>如大地的渴望。

----


### 42 等候
<img src="/assets/wait-42.jpg"/>
<br>
>You smiled and talked to me of nothing and I felt that for this I had been waiting long.

<br>你，对着我，笑而不语，
<br>我，为了这，等候了好久。

----

### 43 拥有 
<img src="/assets/own-43.jpg"/>
<br>
>The fish in the water is silent, the animal on the earth is noisy, the bird in the air is singing,<br>
>But Man has in him the silence of the sea, the noise of the earth and the music of the air.

<br>游鱼在水中沉默，
<br>走兽在陆上喧闹，
<br>飞鸟在空中歌唱。
<br>而人拥有
<br>海的沉默，
<br>地的喧嚣，
<br>天的音乐。

----

### 44 恋恋不舍
<img src="/assets/sad-44.jpg"/>
<br>
>The world rushes on over the strings of the lingering heart making the music of sadness.

<br>世界匆匆掠过
<br>我恋恋不舍的心弦，
<br>奏响忧伤的乐章。

<br>释：
<br>
<br>世界 纷繁忙碌
<br>洪流般 向前奔涌
<br>我心依旧驻留在
<br>昨天 
<br>或温柔或悲伤的 
<br>带着不舍和留恋
<br>
<br>世界 踏过
<br>我依恋的心弦
<br>拉扯 
<br>是一曲带着忧伤的歌。
<br>
<br>我在尘埃里
<br>捡拾散落的温存
<br>不愿让思念
<br>沉入匆匆流年
<br>
<br>世界 翻腾
<br>我驻足眺望
<br>在时光深处
<br>拥抱那一抹光

----

### 45 输赢
<img src="/assets/sword.jpg"/>
<br>
>He has made his weapons his gods.  When his weapons win he is defeated himself.

<br>他拜剑为神。
<br>剑赢了，
<br>他输了。

----

### 46 创造
<img src="/assets/self-sculpture.jpg"/>
<br>
>God finds himself by creating.

上帝在创造中找到自己。

----

### 47 影子
<img src="/assets/shadow.jpg"/>
<br>
>Shadow, with her veil drawn, follows Light in secret meekness, with her silent steps of love.

影子，垂下她的面纱，悄悄地跟随着光，带着她默默的爱意，轻轻的步伐。

----

### 48 星星
<img src="/assets/star-firefly.jpg"/>
<br>
>The stars are not afraid to appear like fireflies.

星星不会担心自己看上去像萤火虫。

----

### 49 感恩
<img src="/assets/grass-field.jpg"/>
<br>
>I thank thee that I am none of the wheels of power but I am one with the living creatures that are crushed by it.

<br>我感谢你，
<br>我没有成为权力的车轮，
<br>而与那些被它碾压的生灵同在。
