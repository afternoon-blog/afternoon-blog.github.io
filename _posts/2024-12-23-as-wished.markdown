译自唐恬的《如愿》

<br>
<br>You are the distant road
<br>The light in the mountain fog
<br>
<br>I am a child
<br>Walking in your eyes
<br>
<br>You are the bright moon and gentle breeze
<br>I am the dream you care for
<br>
<br>Whether we meet or not, I will embrace you for a lifetime
<br>And I will love the world you love
<br>Wish for the smiles you wish for
<br>I stumble as I hold your hand
<br>Please take me to tomorrow
<br>
<br>If you have endured bitterness for my sweetness
<br>I am willing to live out your wishes
<br>
<br>May it not be in vain, may we move bravely forward
<br>In this prosperous world every day
<br>
<br>You are the river of time
<br>The sky ignited by starlight
<br>
<br>I am the admirer
<br>Singing you into a song
<br>
<br>You are where I come from
<br>And where my heart belongs
<br>
<br>All paths in the world will meet you
<br>And I will love the world you love
<br>Wish for the smiles you wish for
<br>I stumble as I hold your hand
<br>Please take me to tomorrow
<br>
<br>If you have endured bitterness for my sweetness
<br>I am willing to live out your wishes
<br>May it not be in vain, may we move bravely forward
<br>In this prosperous world every day
<br>
<br>The mountains and rivers are at peace, life carries on as usual. 
<br>Isn't this the vision you longed for?
<br>Children sleep peacefully in their dreams
<br>Just like you love deeply
<br>
<br>And I will dream of the reunions you dream of
<br>Wish for the forever you wish for
<br>Walk the long roads you walk
<br>To love you like this
<br>
<br>I will see the world you haven't seen
<br>Write the poems you haven't written
<br>The moon at the horizon, the thoughts in my heart
<br>You are always by my side
<br>
<br>With you, I make a promise, a lifetime of clarity
<br>Like your youthful face
<br>
<br>
